Must-Visit Destinations

Explore the
Wonders of Sri Lanka

Every destination is a story — let Ranjan help you live it.

⭐ Rossto Specialty
Kandy & The Hill Country
The cultural capital of Sri Lanka — home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth, the Royal Botanical Gardens of Peradeniya, misty mountains, and the world-famous Kandy Esala Perahera. Rossto's home ground — nobody knows it better.
📍 View on Maps
Nature & Scenery
Ella & Nine Arches
The iconic Nine Arch Bridge, Little Adam's Peak sunrise hikes, and cascading waterfalls through lush tea estates.
📍 View on Maps
Ancient Wonder
Sigiriya Rock Fortress
A UNESCO World Heritage site — climb the 5th-century citadel rising 200m above the jungle plain. Breathtaking and unforgettable.
📍 View on Maps
Colonial History
Galle Fort
Walk Dutch colonial streets within a UNESCO fort on the southern coast — boutiques, cafés, and sunsets over the Indian Ocean.
📍 View on Maps
Tea Country
Nuwara Eliya
"Little England" — rolling tea estates, cool mountain air, and the train journey from Kandy that is ranked among the world's most scenic.
📍 View on Maps
Ocean & Wildlife
Mirissa & Whale Watching
World's best blue whale watching spot, golden beaches, and fresh seafood on the southern coast. A perfect finish to any island tour.
